Users can signup or login and are then redirected to the homepage.

At the homepage, users can view their account, view leaderboards, or select from a variety of games.

If users view their account, they can see their account information.

If users click to view leaderboards, they can toggle between viewing the summary leaderboard or individual game leaderboards.

If users click on a game, they are directed to a playable game that displays how many points will be awarded upon successful completion of the game.
